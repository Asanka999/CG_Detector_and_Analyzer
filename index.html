About Motion Analysis Pro

Motion Analysis Pro is an advanced tool for analyzing human movement using computer vision and biomechanical principles. The system helps athletes, physical therapists, and movement specialists gain insights into motion patterns.

The analysis is powered by MediaPipe and OpenCV, with a focus on accuracy and ease of use.
